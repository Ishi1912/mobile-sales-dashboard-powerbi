# 📊 Mobile Sales Dashboard - Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze mobile sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, transaction patterns, and regional sales trends. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations and filters.

## Project Overview

The **Mobile Sales Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of mobile phone sales data. It enables users to explore sales performance across cities, brands, mobile models, payment methods, customer ratings, and time periods using interactive filters and visualizations.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support strategic business decisions and identify trends affecting revenue and customer engagement.

## Business Insights Derived

### 1. Delhi Emerged as a Major Revenue Contributor

The sales map indicates that metropolitan cities contribute significantly to overall revenue, with Delhi showing one of the highest sales concentrations.

### 2. Customer Ratings Are Predominantly Positive

Most customers rated their purchases highly:

* Rating 5: 27 customers
* Rating 4: 22 customers
* Rating 3: 13 customers
* Rating 2: 7 customers
* Rating 1: 6 customers

This suggests strong customer satisfaction and a positive buying experience.

### 3. Credit Cards and Debit Cards Were the Preferred Payment Methods

Payment distribution revealed:

* Credit Card: 26.67%
* Debit Card: 26.67%
* UPI: 25.33%
* Cash: 21.33%

Digital payment methods accounted for nearly 79% of all transactions, indicating increasing customer preference for cashless payments.

### 4. Apple Generated the Highest Sales Revenue

Brand-wise analysis showed:

| Brand   | Total Sales | Quantity Sold | Transactions |
| ------- | ----------- | ------------- | ------------ |
| Apple   | 15.38M      | 361           | 68           |
| OnePlus | 13.79M      | 354           | 63           |
| Samsung | 14.52M      | 327           | 71           |
| Vivo    | 12.85M      | 332           | 68           |
| Xiaomi  | 11.74M      | 298           | 61           |

Apple emerged as the top-performing brand in terms of revenue generation.

### 5. iPhone SE Was the Best-Selling Mobile Model

Among the mobile models analyzed:

* iPhone SE generated approximately 6.6M in sales.
* OnePlus models followed closely with around 5.6M.
* Samsung Galaxy S21 contributed approximately 5.5M.

This indicates a strong market demand for Apple's products.

### 6. Sales Performance Varied Across the Week

Analysis by day of the week revealed:

* Monday recorded the highest sales (~12.0M).
* Friday followed with ~10.3M.
* Sunday and Tuesday maintained stable performance.
* Wednesday showed comparatively lower sales (~8.7M).

Businesses can utilize these patterns for promotional campaigns and inventory planning.

### 7. Daily Quantity Trends Highlight Demand Fluctuations

The month-wise quantity chart demonstrated fluctuations in purchasing behavior, with certain days exhibiting significantly higher demand. These spikes can help businesses optimize stock management and staffing requirements.
